Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal data is very important to us. We process your data exclusively in accordance with legal regulations (GDPR, TMG).

Contacting Us

If you contact us via the form on the website or by email, your provided data will be stored for six months for the purpose of processing the request and in case of follow-up questions. We do not share this data without your consent.

Your Rights

  • Right to information, correction, and deletion
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdrawal and objection

If you believe that the processing of your data violates data protection law or your data protection rights have otherwise been violated, you can lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority.

Game Data & Authentication

As "Waves of the Fallen" is a multiplayer vampire survivor-like game, we may collect and process gameplay data such as usernames, scores, and multiplayer session information. This data is used solely to provide and improve the gaming experience and is not shared with third parties.

Authentication & JWT

For authentication, "Waves of the Fallen" uses JSON Web Tokens (JWTs) that are generated and stored securely on your device or mobile phone. The JWT is saved in a file on your device and is used to verify your identity when accessing the game and its services. This token is never shared with third parties and is only used for authentication purposes within the game.
