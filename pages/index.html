Waves of the fallen - Full description

Waves of the Fallen is a fast-paced Vampire Survivors-like multiplayer survival game!

Waves of the Fallen is a fast-paced Vampire Survivors-like multiplayer survival game! Team up with up to 4 players or fight alone and survive wave after wave of enemies. Collect gold, level up your character and weapons, and compete for the highest score! The game features simple, colorful graphics and a fun grind-focused gameplay loop — perfect for casual and hardcore players alike.

Features:
  • Multiplayer — play solo or with up to 3 friends!
  • Survive endless enemy waves
  • Collect gold to upgrade characters and weapons
  • Global leaderboard — compete for the highest score!
  • Simple and fun visual style, accessible for all ages

Whether you're looking for a quick session or a long grind — Waves of the Fallen will keep you coming back for more!
